Who Am I?

I am Edd Seabrook, a Software Engineer.

Currently I am a fourth year undergraduate Computer Science student at the University of Southampton. I grew up in West Malling, near Maidstone in Kent, where I attended Maidstone Grammar School.

For as long as I can remember I have had a strong interest in technology and computers, joining my school's programming club when I was eleven. My main interest is in Software development - both the coding and the process that surrounds it. I am particularly interesting in low level programming, with the goal of working on network protocols, operating systems and software infrastructure.

I am also curious about other disciplines - I enjoy learning about economics, business, architecture, and many other subjects. I also have unhealthy obsessions with Google Maps and "self-help" books (Malcolm Gladwell's Outliers being my favourite). Live music and mountain biking are among my other favourite pastimes.
